Aims and Scope

OpenEco Journal highlights the dynamic role of economic sciences in the modern world. We provide an open forum for dialogue between various schools of thought to find balanced ideologies for sustainable global growth.

Thematic Scope:

  • International Economics
  • Finance, Money and Banking
  • Environmental & Green Economy
  • Digital Economy & Innovations
  • Public Finance & Social Policy
  • Sustainable Development Goals

Editorial & Review Process

We implement a strict Double-Blind Peer Review procedure to ensure objectivity and scientific excellence.

  1. Technical Screening: Plagiarism check (Similarity Index must be under 17%, originality above 70%) and formatting verification.
  2. Editorial Assessment: The Editor-in-Chief evaluates the manuscript for thematic relevance and contribution.
  3. External Review: Two independent experts provide a detailed assessment within 30 working days.
  4. Final Decision: Authors receive notification (Accept, Revision, or Reject) along with reviewers' comments.

Guidelines for Authors

Submissions must be original work, following the IMRAD structure (Introduction, Methods, Results, and Discussion). No parallel submissions to other journals are allowed.

Format: Microsoft Word, 12pt Times New Roman, 1.0 spacing.
Length: Min. 3500 words for research papers; 25+ recent sources.
Citation Style: APA 7th Edition (strictly mandatory).
AI Policy: Usage of AI tools must be disclosed in "Materials and Methods".

*Two files are required for submission: Author Bio file and Anonymous Manuscript.

Publication Ethics

In accordance with COPE (Committee on Publication Ethics) standards, we maintain zero tolerance for plagiarism, data falsification, and unethical authorship. All articles, including those from the editorial board, undergo mandatory peer review.
